Purpose

Your purchases will raise money for The Havenly Blue Foundation, the Los Alamitos High School Visual Arts Program, and participating high school students.

Inspiration

The Havenly Blue Foundation advocates for children who are caught in the cycle of abuse and neglect, and challenges all of us to become a voice for these children by spreading awareness. In response, Los Alamitos High School 3D art students have created over 100 unique jewelry pieces that become wearable art with a message of hope!
